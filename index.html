Product manager · AI & integrations · London

I'm a product manager at Carta, where I work on AI-driven document workflows for fund investors — most recently an agentic system that cut document turnaround from over a day to under four hours.

I started out in physics, moved through data engineering, and ended up in product. That path still shapes how I work: understand the workflow in detail, find the friction, then build something that removes effort without adding operational risk. I'm happiest in the space between product strategy and technical execution — AI features, APIs, and messy real-world operations.

Outside work I build side projects, and occasionally write about whatever I'm thinking about.
